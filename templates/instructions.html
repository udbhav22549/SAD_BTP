Instructions for Students

Please read the instructions carefully before starting the experiment.

1. Instructions regarding the test:

The whole experiment will take 60 minutes, consisting of 5 stages:

  • Stage 1: Baseline for EEG (close your eyes) & Speech (read aloud). Just follow the screen.
  • Stage 2: Answer each question verbally within the given timeline.
  • Stage 3: Answer each question verbally while interacting with people around you.
  • Stage 4: Take a 30-minute MCQ test. There is no negative marking.
  • Stage 5: You will be shown a picture; write about it in the provided text box.

Please answer all questions. After completing the test, kindly provide feedback in the feedback section.

2. Using the Empatica Smartwatch:

  • Wear the Empatica smartwatch on your non-dominant hand.
  • Keep your hand as steady as possible throughout the experiment.
  • To start or end the experiment, raise your non-dominant hand and click the corresponding button.

Note: The camera will run in the background throughout the experiment after clicking the Start Test button.
